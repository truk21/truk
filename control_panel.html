Deposits in TrukMining

When you make a deposit, please note that the amount may take between 15 to 20 minutes to reflect in your balance. In some cases, the crediting may be instantaneous. This waiting time is necessary for transaction verification, ensuring that your amount arrives safely in your TrukMining balance.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I start mining?
To start mining, simply sign up on our platform, make an initial deposit, and choose the plan that best suits your needs. Our system will automatically start mining for you. You will earn 1.2% of the initial amount if you invest between 25 USDT and 500 USDT. For investments over 500 USDT, you will start earning 1.5%. Please note that the amount you invest will be locked according to the time you choose: you can opt for plans of 6 months, 9 months, 1 year, or 2 years. You can withdraw your earnings whenever you want or reinvest them to grow your capital.

Additionally, our referral program allows you to earn from 2 USDT to 10 USDT per referral, depending on the capital invested by the referred user. These earnings will be added directly to your balance.

As an additional incentive, we offer special bonuses ranging from 10 USDT to 50 USDT. These bonuses are a reward for users who maintain their capital or investments during the chosen time. Users can withdraw this balance or use it to increase their capital. It is important to note that these bonuses are raffled among users who invest from 50 USDT onwards, and may appear after a month of making the investment. It's our way of thanking you for your trust and commitment to TrukMining!

What withdrawal methods are available?
The only withdrawal method available is through USDT wallet on the TRC20 network. This method guarantees fast and secure transactions for our users.

What is the processing time for withdrawals?
Withdrawals are generally processed within 24-48 business hours. However, times may vary depending on the selected withdrawal method and the necessary security checks.
